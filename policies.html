Policy 4: Password and Account Security Policy (IT-SEC-001)

Section 1 – Scope
Applies to all employees with organizational accounts.

Section 2 – Purpose
To protect systems and data from unauthorized access.

Section 3 – Password Requirements

  • Minimum length: 12 characters
  • Must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol
  • Must not be reused within 12 months

Section 4 – Account Lockout
Accounts are locked after 5 failed login attempts.

Section 5 – Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
MFA is mandatory for all remote logins and privileged accounts.

Section 6 – Non-Compliance
Violations may result in access suspension or disciplinary action.

Policy 3: Procurement Policy (FIN-PR-004)

Section 1 – Scope
Applies to all departments making purchases on behalf of the organization.

Section 2 – Purpose
To ensure purchases are approved, transparent, and cost-effective.

Section 3 – Approval Thresholds

  • Purchases above USD 5,000 require two levels of approval.
  • Purchases above USD 20,000 require finance director approval.

Section 4 – Vendor Policy
All purchases must be made from approved vendors.

Section 5 – Prohibited Practices
Splitting purchases to circumvent approval is strictly prohibited.

Section 6 – Consequences
Violations may result in disciplinary action as per HR policies.

Policy 1: Annual Leave Policy (HR-AL-001)

Section 1 – Scope

This policy applies to all full-time employees of the organization.

Section 2 – Purpose

To define eligibility, accrual, usage, and exceptions for annual leave.

Section 3 – Entitlement

Employees are entitled to 30 calendar days of annual leave per year.

Section 4 – Accrual

Leave accrues 2.5 days per month.

Section 5 – Carryforward

Unused leave may be carried forward for up to six months after the end of the leave year.

Section 6 – Exceptions

Carryforward beyond six months requires written approval from the HR Director.

Section 7 – Procedure

Employee submits leave request via the HR portal.

Manager approves or rejects within 3 business days.

HR validates leave balance and finalizes request.

Policy 2: Remote Work Policy (HR-RW-002)

Section 1 – Scope
Applicable to employees in roles approved by department management.

Section 2 – Purpose
To regulate remote work arrangements and maintain accountability.

Section 3 – Eligibility
Employees must have completed 3 months of probation to be eligible.

Section 4 – Frequency
Remote work is allowed up to two days per week.

Section 5 – Core Hours
Employees must be reachable between 09:00 and 15:00 on remote days.

Section 6 – Equipment
Company-provided devices must be used. Personal devices are not allowed for official work.

Section 7 – Non-Compliance
Failure to comply may result in revocation of remote work privileges.

Policy 5: Travel and Expense Policy (FIN-TR-002)

Section 1 – Scope
Covers all employees submitting travel and expense claims.

Section 2 – Purpose
To define reimbursable expenses and approval workflow.

Section 3 – Reimbursable Expenses

  • Transportation: economy class for flights, mileage at standard rates
  • Accommodation: standard room rate
  • Meals: up to $50 per day

Section 4 – Approval Workflow

  1. Employee submits expense report in the finance portal.
  2. Manager approves or rejects within 5 business days.
  3. Finance team verifies and processes reimbursement.

Section 5 – Exceptions
Any deviation requires written approval from department head.

Section 6 – Non-Compliance
Fraudulent claims will result in disciplinary action.
